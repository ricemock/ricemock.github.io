The RiceBowl Invitational

“Rice hosts its own tournament for college mock trial teams, the RiceBowl Invitational, every fall. Last year in the Inaugural 2018 RiceBowl Invitational, schools from across Texas and beyond came to compete, including teams from Baylor, SMU, Oklahoma, Texas A&M, and more. You can find the full list of attending teams in last year’s tournament at the tabulation summary here. Rice will be hosting the 2019 RiceBowl from October 25-27. If you would like more information about competing or hosting, please contact our tournament director Jonathan Parts at jpp3@rice.edu.”
