Who Does Mock Trial?

  • Actors
  • Debaters
  • Friends

In mock trial we pretend - it’s realism, but with a fair bit of flair. In a typical round you’ll see costumes, hear accents, and maybe even get chills as someone cries on the stand, with full-throated despair over the death of a spouse. Everyone in the room is required to understand and interact with complex characters, and humor, emotion, and creative commitment are central to each and every trial.
