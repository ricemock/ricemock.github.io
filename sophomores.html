Melissa Alter

When she is not memorizing an affidavit, Melissa enjoys reading, writing, and playing with her adorable dog. Alizay Azeem


Alizay Azeem is a Wiess College sophomore, an INFJ (the advocate! let's GOOO attorneys!), and thinks Twitter is a personality trait. After Rice, she's interested in pursuing a career focused on the intersection between human rights, law, and policy. Last year was her first year doing anything like mock trial, but she'll vouch for the activity any day! From embarassingly forgetting her opening statement during her first scrimmage in fall 2019 to advancing to ORCs with her team in the spring and winning an All-Regional attorney award (that freshman year character arc :0), Alizay really enjoyed mock trial and can't wait to see the Rice University Mock Trial program grow the next three years. Outside of RUMT, she's a Rice ACLU officer, part of the Rice Journal for Public Policy team, and occasionally writes for The Rice Thresher. This fall semester she is remote, but she yearns to be back on campus (fondy and chaus specifically) as soon as possible.

Major: Social Policy Analysis, Political Science, Managerial Studies (yeah let's see how long this lasts)
Star Sign: Sagittarius
